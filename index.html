Sub-Intents.

Ten intents. One automated journey. A reimagined upgrade experience designed around the questions customers actually ask.

The Goal
The Shift

From agent-led queues
to instant digital answers.

The goal is to confirm upgrade eligibility, support with guided steps and FAQs.

The Mobile Upgrade intent framework decomposes that journey into ten discrete sub-intents. Each one is mapped to a precise API call, a guided walkthrough, or a direct handoff. The result: answers in seconds, not minutes.

Nine of the ten intents resolve entirely within the automated flow. Only two require human escalation — reserved for the moments that genuinely need a person.
